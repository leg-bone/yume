MEET YUME:

Yume is an approximately 1300 square foot coworking and creative space in Brooklyn on Flatbush Avenue, a major roadway with a decent amount of foot traffic. Located in proximity to Prospect Park, the space has an entrance on street level, and features one bathroom and one closet in addition to the main space. Yume welcomes everyone but also speficially desires to appeal to the following types of customers: college students/ new grads, creatives aiming to build their ideas in the company of like- minded individuals, local makers/artists, and curious people that happen to walk by.
